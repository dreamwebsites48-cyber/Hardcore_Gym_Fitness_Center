General FAQ

What are the gym hours?
We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year for all our members. Experience elite training on your own schedule.
Do you offer personal training?
Yes, we have a team of world-class trainers specializing in strength, HIIT, nutrition, and mobility. You can book a session via our mobile app or at the front desk.
Is there a contract for memberships?
We offer both monthly no-contract options and annual plans for better value. Our goal is to provide flexibility that fits your lifestyle.
What equipment do you have?
We are an official Hammer Strength center. We also feature Life Fitness cardio equipment, rogue squat racks, and a dedicated Olympic lifting platform.
